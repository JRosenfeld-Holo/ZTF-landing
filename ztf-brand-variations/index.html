Concept D

Modern Gradient

  • Contemporary, tech-forward, gradient-rich
  • Vercel / Raycast / Arc browser aesthetic
  • Sunrise palette: darkness (zero) to light (fluency)
  • Feels like a tech product launch, not a training seminar

Ideal use case: The gradient tells the story — from cold/dark (zero) to warm/bright (fluent). Feels premium and current without the corporate stiffness. The sunrise palette is optimistic and aspirational. Strong on social media, email headers, and event materials.

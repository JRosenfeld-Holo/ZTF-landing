# Zero to Fluent — Final Build Brief

## Brand
- **Name:** Zero to Fluent
- **Domain:** zerotofluent.ai
- **Brand Direction:** Clean Authority (Concept A)

## Schedule — Both Days: 10:00 AM – 3:30 PM

### DAY 1: AI Foundations — "From Zero to Comfortable"
**Goal:** By end of Day 1, every attendee can hold a productive conversation with Claude and ChatGPT, write effective prompts, and understand what AI can (and can't) do for their business.

10:00 AM – 10:15 AM: Welcome & Introductions
- Icebreaker: "What's the one thing you wish AI could do for your business?"
- Set expectations for the 2 days
- Quick tech check — make sure everyone's laptop is ready

10:15 AM – 11:15 AM: SESSION 1 — "AI Demystified"
- What AI actually is (and isn't) — no jargon, plain English
- The AI landscape in 2026: Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot — what's what
- Live demo: Watch AI solve a real business problem in real-time
- Why small business owners have a MASSIVE advantage with AI right now

11:15 AM – 11:30 AM: ☕ Coffee Break

11:30 AM – 12:30 PM: SESSION 2 — "Your First AI Conversation"
- Hands-on: Setting up Claude and ChatGPT accounts
- The anatomy of a great prompt — the CRISP framework (Context, Role, Instructions, Specifics, Parameters)
- Workshop exercise: Write 5 prompts that solve YOUR actual business problems
- Common mistakes that make AI give you garbage (and how to fix them)

12:30 PM – 1:30 PM: 🍽️ Lunch Break (Provided)

1:30 PM – 2:30 PM: SESSION 3 — "AI for Your Business — Today"
- Email & communication: Draft emails, proposals, and client responses in seconds
- Content creation: Social media posts, blog outlines, marketing copy
- Research & analysis: Competitive research, market analysis, summarizing documents
- Customer service: Creating templates, FAQ responses, and scripts
- Live exercise: Each attendee automates ONE task they do every week

2:30 PM – 2:45 PM: ☕ Coffee Break

2:45 PM – 3:30 PM: SESSION 4 — "Day 1 Wrap-Up & Homework"
- Group share: "What's the biggest thing you learned today?"
- Homework assignment: Use AI for 3 real business tasks tonight
- Preview of Day 2: "Tomorrow we go from comfortable to fluent"
- Q&A

### DAY 2: Applied AI — "From Comfortable to Fluent"
**Goal:** By end of Day 2, attendees can build AI workflows, use AI for strategic decisions, and have a 90-day implementation plan for their business.

10:00 AM – 10:15 AM: Day 2 Kickoff
- Homework review: "What did you try last night? What worked?"

- Day 2 roadmap

10:15 AM – 11:15 AM: SESSION 5 — "Advanced Prompting & AI Workflows"

11:15 AM – 11:30 AM: ☕ Coffee Break

- Live demo: Build a simple business tool (calculator, form, dashboard) using AI in 15 minutes

12:30 PM – 1:30 PM: 🍽️ Lunch Break (Provided)

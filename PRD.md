## 1. Executive Summary

Zero to Fluent is an in-person AI training workshop business targeting small business owners in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, starting in Frisco, TX. The core product is a 2-day, hands-on workshop ($697) that takes attendees from zero AI experience to practical fluency with Claude, ChatGPT, prompt engineering, vibecoding, and AI automation. Revenue is supplemented by a $199/month online community subscription.

**First Event:** April 21-22, 2026  
**Venue:** Caddo Office Reimagined, 12800 Westridge Blvd, Frisco, TX 75035  
**Capacity:** 40 seats  
**Revenue Target per Event:** $27,880 (40 seats × $697)

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary ICP: DFW Small Business Owner
- **Demographics:** Ages 35-60, located in Frisco/Plano/McKinney/Allen/Prosper/North Dallas
- **Psychographics:** Not technical, feels behind on AI, prefers in-person learning, willing to invest in growth
- **Pain Points:**
  - Competitors are using AI and they're not
  - Tried online courses but never finished them
  - Overwhelmed by the number of AI tools and options
  - Their employees/partners expect them to figure this out
  - They learn better with a teacher than a screen
- **Buying Triggers:**
  - Hearing about AI from peers, news, industry events
  - Losing a client or deal to a more tech-savvy competitor
  - Employee asking about AI tools and not knowing what to say
  - Being told by a consultant or advisor to "start using AI"

## 5. Workshop Curriculum

### Day 1: AI Foundations — "From Zero to Comfortable"

**Goal:** Every attendee can hold a productive conversation with Claude and ChatGPT, write effective prompts, and understand what AI can and can't do for their business.

| Time | Session | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 10:00-10:15 | Welcome & Introductions | Icebreaker, expectations, tech check |
| 10:15-11:15 | Session 1: AI Demystified | What AI is/isn't, 2026 landscape (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot), live demo, small business advantage |
| 11:15-11:30 | ☕ Coffee Break | |
| 11:30-12:30 | Session 2: Your First AI Conversation | Account setup, CRISP prompt framework (Context, Role, Instructions, Specifics, Parameters), write 5 real prompts, common mistakes |
| 12:30-1:30 | 🍽️ Lunch (Provided) | |
| 1:30-2:30 | Session 3: AI for Your Business — Today | Email/proposals, content creation, research/analysis, customer service, automate ONE weekly task |
| 2:30-2:45 | ☕ Coffee Break | |
| 2:45-3:30 | Session 4: Day 1 Wrap-Up | Group share, homework (use AI for 3 real tasks tonight), Day 2 preview, Q&A |

| Time | Session | Description |

| 11:15-11:30 | ☕ Coffee Break | |
| 11:30-12:30 | Session 6: Vibecoding & AI-Built Tools | Building apps without coding, live demo (build a tool in 15 min), hands-on build, "build vs. buy" framework |
| 12:30-1:30 | 🍽️ Lunch (Provided) | |
| 1:30-2:30 | Session 7: AI Agents & Automation | What are agents, real-world small biz examples, automation stack, live demo, DIY vs. hire |
| 2:30-2:45 | ☕ Coffee Break | |
| 2:45-3:30 | Session 8: 90-Day AI Plan & Graduation | Build personalized plan (weeks 1-2 quick wins, weeks 3-4 workflows, months 2-3 strategic), group share, certificate, community invitation, closing |

## 12. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low initial registration | Start outreach 3+ weeks early; leverage referral partners; reduce to 20-seat minimum viability |
| Venue unavailable | Have backup venues identified (WeWork Frisco, Regus Plano) |
| Tech issues at venue | Test all equipment day before; bring backup HDMI cables, hotspot |
| Catering problems | Order from 2 vendors; have backup delivery option (DoorDash for Business) |
| Refund requests | Budget for 5-10% refund rate; the guarantee builds trust and increases registrations |
| Email deliverability | Warm up domains 2-3 weeks before sending; use multiple sending domains |
| Low quiz completion | Monitor drop-off per question; optimize the "bleeding question" within first week |
